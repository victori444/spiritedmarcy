Zeniba's Fortune

The Marcelline Yunas would like to thank the Caisse Desjardins-Bordeaux-Cartierville-Saint Laurent for their contribution put into Marcelline robotics year after year; we recognize and appreciate it deeply. Their sponsorship helps push the team to exceed their expectations for the fifth year of their funding. The trust they put in us motivates us to persevere so that we don’t disappoint and it helps the team do better year after year. Words can not express how grateful we are for everything they do for us.
