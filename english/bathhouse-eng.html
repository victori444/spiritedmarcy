Marcy’s Bathhouse

​

Welcome to Marcy’s Bathhouse, where the Yunas work their blood, sweat and tears, in order to learn various skills. The Yunas come from all around the world. Their establishment was founded in 1967. It is isolated in the woods of Saraguay under the name of Sainte-Marcelline College. When you enter, you’ll find yourself in the spirit realm. 525 Yunas, each devoted to their work, bring Marcy’s Bathhouse to life. It is a French establishment and is constituted entirely of girls. They value friendship, perseverance, honesty and teamwork. Unfortunately, Yubaba, a maleficent spirit, controls Marcy’s Bathhouse taking in her possession all of our goods and knowledge.
